ABOUT

About Dave

Dave is a web developer in Nashville, Tennessee. Thanks to tech savvy parents, he grew up exposed to computers at an early age in Louisville, Kentucky. As a boy he tinkered with BASIC on an Apple IIe, and later lived for the arrival of the ubiquitous AOL CDs in his mailbox.

His career started in music business, but various roles have pushed his interests back into IT and programming. From discussing how the internet affects intellectual property rights with Congress, publishing weekly e-newsletters for SOLID Nashville, managing web services and email outreach for the Nashville Songwriters Association, to research and support in the mobile telecom industry, Dave has acquired a diverse set of business and technical skills. He started making websites in 2011.

Today Dave is studying full stack web development, growing his coding skills, and learning new techniques for building beautiful, responsive web applications. He is always looking for new collaborators and challenges!
